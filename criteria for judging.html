Criteria for Judging:

INNOVATIVE IDEAS (30%)

Creativity & Innovation: How creative/innovative are the solutions proposed? Practicality: How the tools/techniques proposed relevant in solving the problems?

BUSINESS FEASIBILITY (30%)

Market Potential: Does the solution address a significant market need or opportunity? Revenue Model: Is the model plan for generating revenue realistic?

SOCIAL & ENVIRONMENTAL IMPACT (20%)

Social and Environmental Impact: How does the solution contribute to positive social or environmental outcomes?

TEAM MANAGEMENT (10%)

Collaboration: How effectively did the team work together to develop the solution? Roles and Responsibilities: Were team roles well-defined, and did team members contribute effectively?

PRESENTATION & COMMUNICATION (10%)

Clarity and effectiveness of the presentation. Ability to articulate and communicate the solution persuasively.
